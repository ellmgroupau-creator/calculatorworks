Business calculator guide

Business calculators can help with pricing, profit margins, break-even points, ratios, markups, and operating decisions. They are best used to test simple scenarios before building a more detailed spreadsheet or financial model.

Important: Business results should be treated as planning estimates, not accounting advice. Always check formulas, costs, taxes, and assumptions against your own business records.
