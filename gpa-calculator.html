GPA Calculator

Calculate a simple or weighted GPA using letter grades or grade points, with optional credit hours for each course.

GPA Calculator

Enter grades separated by commas. Use letters like A, B+, C- or points like 4.0, 3.7, 3.0. Add matching credits if you want a weighted GPA.

Result
Enter a value to begin

Formula

GPA = total grade points ÷ total courses
Weighted GPA = sum of (grade points × credits) ÷ total credits

Example: A, B+, A- with credits 3, 3, 4 gives a weighted GPA of 3.67.

Worked examples

A, B, C = GPA 3.00

A, B+, A- with credits 3, 3, 4 = weighted GPA 3.67

4.0, 3.3, 3.7, 2.7 = GPA 3.43
