How this calculator works

This calculator estimates a normal monthly payment and converts it into a biweekly equivalent.

Use this result as a planning estimate. Real lending, payroll, tax, health or construction outcomes can vary based on official rules, local costs and personal circumstances.

Formula

Biweekly payment = monthly payment × 12 ÷ 26

Worked example

A $500,000 loan at 6.5% over 30 years has a monthly payment estimate, then a biweekly equivalent based on 26 payments per year.
