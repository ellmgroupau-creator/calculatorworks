How it works

days = |end date − start date|

The calculator counts the total number of calendar days between the two dates. The result is always a positive number regardless of date order.

Worked examples

1 Jan 2026 to 31 Dec 2026 → 364 days

14 Feb 2026 to 21 Apr 2026 → 66 days

1 Mar 2026 to 1 Jun 2026 → 92 days

FAQ

What does this calculator count?

It counts the number of days between the two dates you enter so you can compare timelines, deadlines, or elapsed periods more accurately.

When is this better than estimating by month?

It is better whenever the exact number of days matters, because calendar months vary in length.

Can I use it for billing periods or trip planning?

Yes. It is useful for subscriptions, leave calculations, project schedules, travel timing, and due-date checks.
