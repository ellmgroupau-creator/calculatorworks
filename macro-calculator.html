Estimate daily macronutrient grams from a calorie target and protein/fat preferences.

Formula

carbs = (calories − protein × 4 − fat × 9) ÷ 4.

Worked example

2,400 calories with 160 g protein and 70 g fat leaves about 255 g carbohydrates.

How to use this calculator
