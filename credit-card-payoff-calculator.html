How to use payoff results

Run the calculation with your current payment first, then test a higher payment to see how much faster the balance could fall. This makes the cost of minimum-only repayment easier to understand.

Before relying on the result: Credit card interest can depend on purchase rates, cash advance rates, interest-free periods, fees, promotional rates, and payment allocation rules.
