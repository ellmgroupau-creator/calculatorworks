Como esta calculadora funciona

The calculator uses a standard amortising loan formula. It converts the annual rate to a monthly rate, spreads payments across the full loan term, and estimates the fixed monthly principal-and-interest repayment.

Resultados are estimates only. Real outcomes can vary because of fees, repayment timing, tax rules, lender criteria, compounding methods, and your personal situation.

Quando é útil

Use it to compare loan sizes, interest rates, and repayment terms before speaking with a lender or broker.
