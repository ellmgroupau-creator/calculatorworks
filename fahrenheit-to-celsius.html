When to use this Fahrenheit to Celsius converter

Fahrenheit is still used in weather reports, recipes, and appliance settings in some countries, while Celsius is standard elsewhere. This converter helps you switch those numbers into metric temperature values quickly.

  • Convert weather forecasts and travel temperatures into Celsius.
  • Switch recipe, oven, and food safety temperatures from Fahrenheit into metric values.

Fahrenheit to Celsius FAQ

What is the formula for Fahrenheit to Celsius?

The formula is Celsius = (Fahrenheit − 32) × 5/9. The calculator handles it automatically and rounds the result cleanly.

What Fahrenheit temperature equals 0 °C?

32 °F equals 0 °C, which is the freezing point of water at standard pressure.

Is this useful for recipes and ovens?

Yes. It helps when recipe instructions use Fahrenheit but your oven and kitchen tools use Celsius.
