Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains, in general terms, how CalculatorWorks may collect and use information when you visit the site. It is written to support a simple calculator website that may use analytics and carefully limited advertising.

Last updated: April 2026

Website usage data

Like many websites, CalculatorWorks may use basic analytics, server logs, and similar tools to understand site usage, improve performance, and monitor traffic patterns. This may include general technical information such as browser type, pages visited, and approximate location data.

Cookies and advertising

CalculatorWorks may use cookies or similar technologies for website functionality, analytics, and advertising. Third-party advertising providers, including Google, may use cookies to help serve and measure ads where enabled.

If Google AdSense or similar advertising is enabled, ads may be personalised or non-personalised depending on your region, browser settings, consent requirements, and the provider configuration in use at the time.

Contact information

If you contact CalculatorWorks directly, any details you provide, such as your name or email address, may be used only to respond to your enquiry or review your feedback.

Third-party services

Some parts of the site may rely on third-party services such as analytics, hosting, or advertising platforms. Those services may handle data according to their own policies.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the site or its services. Continued use of the site after updates means you accept the revised policy.

How to ask questions about privacy

If you have a privacy question about analytics, cookies, advertising, or information submitted by email, use the details on the Contact page.
