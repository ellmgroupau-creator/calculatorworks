Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains, in general terms, how CalculatorWorks may collect and use information when you visit the site. It is written to support a simple calculator website that may use analytics and carefully limited page content.

Last updated: May 2026

Website usage data

Like many websites, CalculatorWorks may use basic analytics, server logs, and similar tools to understand site usage, improve performance, and monitor traffic patterns. This may include general technical information such as browser type, pages visited, and approximate location data.

Cookies and page content

CalculatorWorks may use cookies or similar technologies for essential site functions, measurement, and page content. Third-party page content providers, including Google, may use cookies to help serve, limit, personalise, or measure ads where page content is enabled.

Google and its partners may use information from visits to this and other websites to show ads that are more relevant to users, subject to consent settings, browser settings, regional requirements, and Google’s own policies.

Your choices: You can accept or reject optional cookies using the banner on this site. You can also manage browser cookie settings and review Google’s page content controls at Google Ads Settings.

Third-party page content vendors

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to CalculatorWorks or other websites. These vendors may collect or receive information from this site and elsewhere on the internet and use that information to provide measurement services and targeted or contextual page content.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites and apps that use its services at Google’s partner sites policy page.

Calculator inputs

CalculatorWorks is designed so calculations run in your browser. The numbers you enter into calculator fields are not intentionally collected by CalculatorWorks as form submissions. Standard server logs, analytics, page content, or browser technologies may still process general usage information as described in this policy.

Contact information

If you contact CalculatorWorks directly, any details you provide, such as your name or email address, may be used only to respond to your enquiry or review your feedback.

Third-party services

Some parts of the site may rely on third-party services such as analytics, hosting, or page content platforms. Those services may handle data according to their own policies.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the site or its services. Continued use of the site after updates means you accept the revised policy.

How to ask questions about privacy

If you have a privacy question about analytics, cookies, page content, or information submitted by email, use the details on the Contact page.
