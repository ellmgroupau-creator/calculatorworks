General enquiries

For general questions, calculator suggestions, corrections, or website feedback, email contact@calculatorworks.io.

Please include the page URL and a short description of your request so it can be reviewed efficiently.

Response note: CalculatorWorks is a growing independent calculator website, so replies may not be immediate. Feedback that improves calculator accuracy, usability, or clarity is prioritised.

Report a calculator issue

If you believe a calculator result, formula explanation, link, or page content needs attention, please include:

  • the page name or URL,
  • the inputs you used,
  • the result you expected, and
  • a brief explanation of the problem.

This helps keep the tools accurate, useful, and transparent for future visitors.

Response expectations

We may not be able to respond to every message immediately, but we do review feedback to help improve the site over time.

Best way to help: Include the page URL or calculator name, the values you entered, and what looked wrong.

Advertising note: We do not offer paid placement that changes calculator results or editorial recommendations.

What to contact us about

  • Calculator bugs or confusing page content.
  • Suggestions for new calculators or clearer explanations.
  • General site feedback about usability, trust, or page quality.
