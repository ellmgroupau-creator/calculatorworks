Wallpaper Calculator

Estimate how many wallpaper rolls may be needed for a room or wall section.

Result
Enter values to begin

Formula

rolls needed = wall area × (1 + waste %) ÷ coverage per roll.

Worked example

28 m² of wall area with 5.2 m² rolls and 15% waste needs about 7 rolls.

How to use this calculator

Use manufacturer roll coverage where possible and allow extra for pattern matching, trimming and mistakes.
