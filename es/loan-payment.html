Cómo funciona esta calculadora

The calculator applies the standard amortising payment formula for fixed-rate loans. It estimates the regular monthly repayment, total repayment, and total interest over the term.

Resultados are estimates only. Real outcomes can vary because of fees, repayment timing, tax rules, lender criteria, compounding methods, and your personal situation.

Cuándo es útil

Use it for personal loans, equipment loans, and other fixed-payment loans where you want a fast repayment estimate.
