Contractor Rate Calculator

Use this calculator to estimate the hourly rate needed to reach a target annual income after allowing for expenses, unpaid time and fewer billable hours.

Fórmula

contractor hourly rate = target income × (1 + overhead %) ÷ annual billable hours

Ejemplo práctico

A $120,000 target income, 30 billable hours per week and a 25% overhead buffer gives an estimated rate of about $96.15 per hour.
