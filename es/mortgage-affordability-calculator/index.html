Fórmula

maximum loan = payment × ((1 + monthly rate)^months − 1) ÷ (monthly rate × (1 + monthly rate)^months)

Ejemplo práctico

A $2,800 monthly budget at 6.5% over 30 years supports an estimated loan of about $442,600 before adding any down payment.
