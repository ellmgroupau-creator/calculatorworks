Fence Calculator

Use this calculator to estimate the number of fence panels and posts needed for a straight or total combined fence length.

Fórmula

panels = ceiling(length ÷ panel width); posts = ceiling(length ÷ post spacing) + 1

Ejemplo práctico

A 36 m fence with 2.4 m panels needs about 15 panels and 16 posts.
