How this calculator works

This simple comparison adds monthly ownership costs to the mortgage payment and compares that with rent plus renter costs. It is not a full investment model, but it gives a quick cash-flow comparison.

Results are estimates only. Real outcomes can vary because of fees, repayment timing, tax rules, lender criteria, compounding methods, and your personal situation.

When this is useful

Use it as an early planning tool when deciding whether the monthly cost of buying is close to, above, or below the cost of renting.
