About CalculatorWorks

CalculatorWorks is a growing collection of free online calculators designed to make everyday calculations quicker, easier, and easier to trust.

What we do

We build straightforward calculators for common tasks such as percentages, unit conversions, and basic finance topics. Our goal is to keep tools simple to use, fast to load, and easy to understand.

Our approach

We focus on practical tools, clear formulas, and concise guidance. CalculatorWorks is intended as a helpful starting point for everyday planning, budgeting, conversions, and maths checks.

User-first design: Pages are built to load fast, work without signups, and keep the calculator or explanation as the main focus.

Monetisation approach: If advertising is added, placements will stay limited, clearly separated from content, and should not block results or key navigation.

Important note

While we aim to keep information and calculations accurate, CalculatorWorks is provided for general informational use and should not replace professional advice for legal, financial, medical, tax, or engineering decisions.

What you can expect on the site

  • Clear links to About, Contact, Privacy Policy, and Terms on every page.
  • Calculator pages that explain the purpose of the tool before any future advertising appears.
  • Simple static pages without forced account creation, popups, or misleading buttons.
