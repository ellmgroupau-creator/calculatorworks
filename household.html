Household calculator guide

Household calculators help estimate materials for everyday projects such as paint, tile, flooring, concrete, room size, and home planning. They are useful for early budgeting and shopping lists.

Important: Always allow for waste, offcuts, uneven surfaces, product coverage variation, and local building requirements before purchasing materials.
