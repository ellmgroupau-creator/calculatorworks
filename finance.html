Finance calculators FAQ

Which finance calculator should I use for a home loan?

Use the Mortgage Calculator for monthly repayment, total repayment, and total interest estimates on a home loan.

What is the difference between margin and markup?

Margin is profit as a percentage of selling price, while markup is the added amount as a percentage of cost. CalculatorWorks has separate tools for both.

Can I compare borrowing and saving scenarios on the site?

Yes. Use the Loan Repayment Calculator or Mortgage Calculator for borrowing, then compare them with the Savings Calculator for growth estimates.
