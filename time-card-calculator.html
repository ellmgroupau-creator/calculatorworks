Time Card Calculator

Calculate total paid hours for a repeated shift pattern after unpaid breaks.

Result
Enter values to begin

Formula

paid hours = (end time − start time − break hours) × days.

Worked example

9:00 to 17:30 with a 30-minute break over 5 days gives 40 paid hours.

How to use this calculator

Enter times as decimal hours, such as 17.5 for 5:30 pm, and use the result as a quick payroll planning estimate.
