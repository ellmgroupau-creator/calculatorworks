Percentage calculator FAQ

How do I calculate X% of a number?

Multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100. For example, 15% of 200 is 30.

When should I use this instead of percent change?

Use this calculator when you have one percentage and one base number. Use Percent Change Calculator when you are comparing an old value against a new value.

Can I use this for discounts and tips?

Yes. You can calculate the discount amount or tip amount here, then use the Discount Calculator if you also want the final sale price.

Why is this different from percentage increase or decrease?

This page gives you the raw percentage amount from one base value. Increase and decrease calculators go a step further by applying that amount back to the original number.
