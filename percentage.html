How Percentage Calculations Work

A percentage represents a fraction of 100. To find X% of a number, multiply the number by X and divide by 100. Percentage increase and decrease apply this idea in reverse — adding or subtracting the calculated amount from the original value.

result = (percentage ÷ 100) × number

These formulas are used in finance, shopping, statistics, and everyday life. Each calculator above shows its own worked examples so you can double-check your results.

Percentage calculators FAQ

What is the easiest way to calculate a percentage?

Multiply the number by the percentage and divide by 100. If you want the answer instantly, use the Percentage Calculator.

Which calculator should I use for sale prices?

Use the Discount Calculator when you know the original price and discount percentage and want the final sale price plus savings.

What is the difference between percent change and percentage increase?

Percent change measures the difference between two values and can go up or down. Percentage increase only handles upward movement from the starting value.
