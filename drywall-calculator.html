Drywall Calculator

Plan plasterboard or drywall sheet quantities for walls and rooms using simple area inputs.

Result
Enter values to begin

Formula

sheets needed = wall area × (1 + waste %) ÷ sheet area.

Worked example

42 m² of wall area using 2.88 m² sheets and 10% waste needs about 17 sheets.

How to use this calculator

Measure total wall area, subtract large openings if appropriate, then include waste for cuts and breakage.
