How this calculator works

current ratio = current assets ÷ current liabilities. The calculator keeps the arithmetic visible and avoids unnecessary assumptions. Change the inputs to compare scenarios before making a decision.

For money decisions, use the result as a planning estimate. Real outcomes can vary because of fees, compounding rules, repayment timing, tax treatment, lender criteria, and personal circumstances.

When this is useful

This calculator is useful when you want a fast estimate, a comparison between options, or a starting point before checking exact figures from a bank, lender, employer, accountant, or adviser.
