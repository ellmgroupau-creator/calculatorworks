When to use this km to miles converter

Kilometres are standard in many countries, while miles are still widely used for road distances, running targets, and travel planning in others. This converter helps you compare distances without mental maths.

  • Convert road trip and route distances when maps, signs, or apps use different unit systems.
  • Translate race and workout distances between kilometres and miles for training plans.

KM to miles FAQ

How many miles are in one kilometre?

One kilometre equals about 0.621371 miles. Multiply kilometres by 0.621371 to estimate the distance in miles.

Is this useful for running and walking plans?

Yes. It is helpful for converting 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon distances into miles.

Can I use it for road travel?

Yes. It works well for trip planning, map comparisons, and understanding overseas distance signage.
