How this calculator works

This simplified comparison focuses on monthly cash flow. It does not model tax treatment, home-price growth, investment returns or transaction costs.

Change one input at a time to compare scenarios clearly. This is useful for planning, but it should not be treated as a final quote, tax calculation, lending decision, employment advice or professional recommendation.

Formula

Monthly ownership estimate = mortgage payment + taxes + insurance + maintenance + other ownership costs

Worked example

If rent is $2,800 and estimated ownership costs are $4,300 per month, buying costs about $1,500 more per month before equity and price-growth assumptions.
