How to choose the right finance calculator

Start with the decision you need to make, then use the calculator that matches the question. For home loans, begin with the mortgage calculator, then move to amortization, refinance or payoff calculators. For income planning, use salary, hourly, paycheck and withholding tools together. For debt and savings, compare repayment speed, interest cost, contribution amount and time horizon.
