When to use this inches to centimetres converter

Inches still show up on screens, imported tools, furniture specs, and craft instructions, while centimetres are standard in many countries. This converter helps you turn imperial measurements into a metric value you can use confidently.

Inches to CM FAQ

How many centimetres are in one inch?

One inch equals exactly 2.54 centimetres. That exact definition makes the conversion reliable for everyday use and technical work.

Is this good for monitor and TV sizing?

Yes. It helps when a screen size is shown in inches but you want the dimension in centimetres for planning or comparison.

Can I use this for school and craft projects?

Yes. It works well for classroom maths, sewing, woodworking, and general measuring tasks.
