When to use this metres to feet converter

Metric measurements are standard in many countries, but feet and inches still appear in real estate listings, gym and sports stats, and imported product descriptions. This converter is useful when you need to switch a metric height or dimension into a more familiar imperial format.

  • Convert metric height into feet and inches for international forms, fitness profiles, and sports listings.
  • Check room and furniture sizes when a building plan is metric but the product details are imperial.
  • Use the reference table for quick comparisons, then run the calculator when you need exact feet and inches.

Metres to feet FAQ

How many feet are in one metre?

One metre equals about 3.28084 feet. The calculator then converts the decimal part into inches for a familiar feet-and-inches result.

Why not just show decimal feet?

People usually read height and similar measurements in feet and inches, so splitting the result makes it easier to use.

Can I use this for building measurements?

Yes. It works for ceiling heights, furniture dimensions, room sizes, and other metre-based measurements.
