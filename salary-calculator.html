Salary Calculator

Estimate monthly, fortnightly, weekly, and hourly pay from an annual salary figure.

Salary Calculator

Enter an annual salary and the number of work hours per week to estimate common pay intervals.

Result
Enter a value to begin

Formula

monthly pay = annual salary ÷ 12
fortnightly pay = annual salary ÷ 26
weekly pay = annual salary ÷ 52
hourly pay = annual salary ÷ (52 × hours per week)

Example: An $85,000 annual salary over a 38-hour week is about $7,083.33 monthly, $3,269.23 fortnightly, $1,634.62 weekly, and $43.02 hourly.

Worked examples

$60,000 salary, 38 hours/week = $5,000.00 monthly, $30.36 hourly

$85,000 salary, 38 hours/week = $7,083.33 monthly, $43.02 hourly

$120,000 salary, 40 hours/week = $10,000.00 monthly, $57.69 hourly
