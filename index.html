How to use this calculator

  1. Enter the number of feet.
  2. Choose how many decimal places you want.
  3. Click Calculate.
  4. Read the result in metres.

Formula

metres = feet × 0.3048

Example: 10 feet × 0.3048 = 3.048 metres.

Worked examples

5 ft = 1.524 m

10 ft = 3.048 m

20 ft = 6.096 m
