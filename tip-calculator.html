Tip Calculator

Work out the tip amount, total bill, and per-person split with a simple and accurate tip calculator.

Tip Calculator

Enter the bill amount, tip percentage, and number of people to split the bill.

Result
Enter a value to begin

Formula

tip = bill × (tip percentage ÷ 100)
total = bill + tip
per person = total ÷ number of people

Example: A $120 bill with a 15% tip split 3 ways gives a $18 tip, $138 total, and $46 per person.

Worked examples

$80 bill, 10% tip, 2 people = $8.00 tip, $88.00 total, $44.00 each

$120 bill, 15% tip, 3 people = $18.00 tip, $138.00 total, $46.00 each

$200 bill, 20% tip, 4 people = $40.00 tip, $240.00 total, $60.00 each
