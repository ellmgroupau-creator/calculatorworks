Celsius to Fahrenheit FAQ

What is the formula for Celsius to Fahrenheit?

The formula is Fahrenheit = Celsius × 9/5 + 32. The calculator does this instantly for any temperature.

Is 0 °C the freezing point of water?

Yes. 0 °C equals 32 °F, which is the freezing point of water at standard pressure.

Is this useful for cooking and ovens?

Yes. It is useful when recipes are written in Celsius but your oven or appliance uses Fahrenheit.
