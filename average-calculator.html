Formula

average = sum of all numbers ÷ count of numbers

Example: (10 + 20 + 30) ÷ 3 = 20.000

Worked examples

10, 20, 30 → Average = 20.000

5, 15, 25, 35 → Average = 20.000

72, 85, 91, 68, 77 → Average = 78.600
