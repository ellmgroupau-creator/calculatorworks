Average Calculator

Formula

average = sum of all numbers ÷ count of numbers

Example: (10 + 20 + 30) ÷ 3 = 20.000

Worked examples

10, 20, 30 → Average = 20.000

5, 15, 25, 35 → Average = 20.000

72, 85, 91, 68, 77 → Average = 78.600

FAQ

What kind of average does this page calculate?

This page calculates the arithmetic mean, which adds all numbers together and divides by how many numbers are in the list.

Can I use it for grades, budgets, or prices?

Yes. It works well for scores, expenses, prices, and other numeric lists where you want a quick overall average.

What if one value is much larger than the others?

A large outlier can pull the mean up or down. If you need the middle value instead, a median-style tool would be more suitable.
