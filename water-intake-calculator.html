How this calculator works

This calculator estimates baseline fluid needs and adds an allowance for exercise minutes.

Use this result as a planning estimate. Real lending, payroll, tax, health or construction outcomes can vary based on official rules, local costs and personal circumstances.

Formula

Water target ≈ weight × 35 ml + exercise allowance

Worked example

80 kg with 45 minutes of exercise estimates a higher daily fluid target than baseline alone.
