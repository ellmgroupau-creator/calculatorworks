Mortgage Calculator

Estimate monthly mortgage repayments plus total repayment and total interest using a standard amortisation formula.

Mortgage Calculator

Enter the mortgage amount, annual interest rate, and loan term to estimate monthly repayments.

Result
Enter a value to begin

Formula

monthly repayment = P × r × (1 + r)^n ÷ ((1 + r)^n - 1)
total repayment = monthly repayment × n
total interest = total repayment - principal

Example: A $500,000 mortgage at 6.2% over 30 years has an estimated monthly repayment of about $3,062.34.

Worked examples

$350,000 at 5.8% over 25 years = about $2,212.46 per month

$500,000 at 6.2% over 30 years = about $3,062.34 per month

$650,000 at 6% over 30 years = about $3,897.08 per month
