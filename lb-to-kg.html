When to use this lb to kg converter

Pounds are common in the United States and in many sports, retail, and shipping contexts, while kilograms are standard in most other places. This converter helps you translate weight values cleanly into metric units.

  • Convert body weight, gym targets, or sports stats from pounds into kilograms.
  • Check parcel, luggage, and freight weights when your source is imperial but your paperwork uses metric units.
  • Use the quick reference table to estimate common weights without extra calculator steps.

LB to KG FAQ

How many kilograms are in one pound?

One pound equals about 0.453592 kilograms. Multiply the pound value by 0.453592 to convert it to kilograms.

Is this useful for health and medical forms?

Yes. Many international forms and medical records use kilograms even if the original measurement was taken in pounds.

Can I use this for delivery weights?

Yes. It is useful for checking shipping labels, freight documentation, and baggage allowances.
